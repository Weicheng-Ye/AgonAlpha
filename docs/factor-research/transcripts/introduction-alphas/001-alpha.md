## Description

This video explains what an Alpha is and how it is created and tested. The video also describes how Alphas are used to create market neutral equity portfolios

HIGHLIGHTS GENERATED WITH THE HELP OF AI

•	📊 An Alpha is a mathematical model that seeks to predict the performance of financial instruments.
•	💼 The development of an Alpha involves creating a fictitious portfolio and deciding on the distribution of money among various financial instruments.
•	🛡️ The aim is to ensure downside protection while maintaining good returns.
•	⚖️ The approach used is equity long-short market-neutral investing, which aims to minimize market exposure and maximize simulated profit from changes in the spread between two stocks.
•	🔄 Alpha development starts with an idea, implemented and refined iteratively through statistical analysis.
